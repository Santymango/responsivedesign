Amazonas Tour

Colombia / Amazonas Region

Date: 01/07/2021

Info and details: www.colombiasafetravel.com/amazonastour

The Amazon River that millions of years ago flowed towards the Pacific Ocean but after the formation of the Andes mountain range was left without an outlet to the sea and after forming countless lakes it managed to find another outlet but towards the Atlantic Ocean on the opposite side ; it dumps up to a fifth of the world's fresh water into the Atlantic due to its flow; he is the king in a jungle that is home to hundreds of species of reptiles, mammals, birds, thousands of types of plants and indigenous reserves with millenary tradition from the Colombian side.

Whale Watching Tour

Colombia / Pacific coast

Date: 01/07/2020

Info and details: Click Here

In season from July 15th to October 15th, humpback whales arrive at the Pacific Region in Colombia from Antarctica, to mate and have their babies and the best place to appreciate this wonder is the Pacific coast, where we will be guiding you to one of the best experience in Colombia.
